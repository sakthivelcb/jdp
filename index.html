Senior Direct Sales Executive - SimSci Germany & Eastern Europe m/f SYS

  • Recruitment Genius
  • Atlanta, GA
  • 1/4/2016
  • Apply now

Job Snapshot

Full-Time Travel - Up to 25% Experience - 0 to 5 year(s) Degree - 4 Year Degree Marketing, Sales, Entry Level Sales - Marketing, Accounting - Finance, Advertising

Job Description

New Business Sales Executive - GBP£50,000 + OTE, Benefits - MS Gold Partner

This Microsoft Gold ERP partner is the UK's leading provider of business management solutions to the distribution, wholesale, manufacturing and fashion industries, supporting more than 200 clients with their business critical IT systems.

The Microsoft Dynamics Powerhouse is now looking for a New Business Sales Executive to generate and convert more business for the Microsoft Dynamics NAV software in the Wholesale, Distribution & Manufacturing sectors.

You will have a sales target of £600k margin contribution the right Sales Exec will have had a proven track record with software sales experience! Creative negotiating skills are key to this role and the confidence to negotiate at all levels of the business - However, a level of flexibility is required in terms of dealing with both small and large opportunities - a good understanding of business process linked with good communication skills is essential.

Skills and Experience Required;

* Proven experience of success in the sale of a complex integrated ERP software solution
* A record of achievement with aggressive sales targets
* Experience in closing deals of up to £500k
* Previously sold to the Fashion, Wholesale, Distribution & Manufacturing sector
* Ideally worked for; Microsoft NAV, SAP Business One, Sage, Access, Epicor or any other leading Wholesale, Distribution & Manufacturing software solution providers
* Management of complex solution sales cycles, ideally with a strong level of structure underpinning the approach, i.e. Target Account Selling.
* Strong educational background required, preferably of degree calibre or equivalent

The right candidate;

* Must be able to demonstrate that you are a true Solution sales person
* Can demonstrate accurate pipeline management and reporting capabilities
* Must have good IT skills : Excel, Word, Power Point
* Will have experience of making presentations of complex solutions to all levels - from board level down - You must be a confident, articulate presenter.

As Dynamics NAV sales executive, you should be able to present to any level of the business and be self-motivated with an energetic approach and the ability to work well within a team as well as on own initiative. Excellent oral and written communication skills essential.

This is a career-elevating opportunity for a ERP Sales Executive who would like to be placed at the forefront of the technology curve, in a challenging work environment with an established and reputable solutions provider - If successful you can look forward to a basic salary of £50,000 + OTE, annual bonus, and pension.
